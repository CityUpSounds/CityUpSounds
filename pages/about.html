About

Thankyou for visiting my site. I have been blown away by the feedback and reactions I have received.

This site was started basically to share my passion of sounds and recordings with an entire world of people whom share a similar interest.

My interests initially began mid 2016(ish) with my first sound effects recordings. Recorded only with the smartphone I had at the time, you would appreciate the quality was fairly raw, but was exactly enough to spark the passion I share with so many others today. Along the way I have improved my recording equipment so as to capture a more detailed sound quality. Now as my collection of recordings grows, I share them with you.

I hope to continue to grow both this site and my collection of recordings, while sharing my passion for sound.

Images, Video and Audio

I physicially take the photos, capture the video and record the audio myself. Yep. The images used on this website (exceptions noted below), the video embeded and the audio have been taken by me using my camera / GoPro / recording equipment. I feel the images and video, just like the sound recordings, are such an important part of the experience to help reflect that time; that place.

I try to capture images which reflect the emotions of the location and in a way, the emotions of the day, whether be dark or joyfull.

PNG - Mostly edited using GIMP.

SVG - Mostly edited using SVG-Edit.

MP4 - Edited using Shotcut.

WAV and MP3 - Mostly edited using Audacity

HTML and CSS

This site has been built by myself line by line, file by file. I scripted the site based on HTML and CSS. I specifically chose not to include JavaScript. Quite simply, do not need it. Media queries in the CSS and utalising flexbox allows the site to be repsonsive / mobile friendly.

Scripting software used is Visual Studio Code

Building this site myself has allowed me the opportunitity to learn many concepts of HTML and CSS. Influences and guidance has mostly come from YouTube, web searches, trial and error. I am comfortable acknowledging there shall be errors in this site or that the script could have been written more efficient. Thats part of the learning experience. Maybe one day I may rebuild the site from scratch again with an improved style... Maybe?
